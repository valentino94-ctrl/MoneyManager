# Money Manager Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** July 21, 2026

Money Manager is a personal-finance application designed to work primarily on
your device. This policy explains how the app handles information.

## Information handled by the app

Money Manager processes the financial information that you enter, including
account names and balances, transactions, categories, budgets, recurring
schedules, saving goals, and notes. This information is stored in the app's
private storage on your device.

The app may also store your preferences, such as currency, theme, notification,
and security settings.

## How information is used

The app uses this information only to provide its features, including balance
calculations, budgets, reports, reminders, recurring schedules, saving goals,
and the balance widget.

## Sharing and sale of information

Money Manager does not sell your information. The current version does not send
your financial records to the developer or to an advertising or analytics
service.

When you choose to export, back up, or share information, Android may provide
the resulting file to a location or application that you select. The privacy
practices of that destination are outside Money Manager's control.

## Device backup

Money Manager does not opt its private app data into Android cloud backup or
device-to-device transfer. You can create a backup manually from Settings and
choose where to store it. You are responsible for protecting and deleting
manually exported backup files.

## Permissions and device features

Money Manager may use:

- Notifications to deliver reminders you enable.
- Biometric authentication to lock access to the app when you enable that
  feature. Biometric credentials are verified by Android and are not available
  to Money Manager.
- File access selected through Android to import or export data.
- A home-screen widget to show balance information when you add the widget.

## Retention and deletion

Information remains on your device until you delete it in the app, clear the
app's storage, or uninstall the app. Exported files and platform-managed backups
must be deleted separately from their respective locations.

## Security

Money Manager relies on Android's application sandbox to protect locally stored
data. You may optionally enable the biometric app lock. No method of storage is
guaranteed to be completely secure, so you should protect access to your device
and keep any exported files in a secure location.

## Children's privacy

Money Manager is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect
personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated when the app's features or data practices change. The
effective date at the top of this page will identify the latest version.
